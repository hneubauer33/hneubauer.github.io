Barbara Jones-Hugo

Barbara Jones-Hogu was born and raised in Chicago, Illinois. She studied at Howard University, the School of the Art Institute of Chicago, and the Institute of Design at IIT, receiving an MS degree with a concentration in printmaking. She was a member of COBRA and in 1968 she helped found AfriCOBRA. She has created prints and films that are centered around African diasporic aesthetics and has contributed to major projects like Chicagoâ€™s Wall of Respect. Her work has been exhibited in museums across the United States. She returned to school in her 70s studying filmmaking. Jones-Hogu passed away at the age of 79 in 2017.
